 They're not textures, they're models that use textures. And actually, only objects binded with bones with Rotate in their name rotate along the screen. (i.e. Peach's Castle, Mushroom hills, Volcano, etc.) They use a single-digit number that define how fast the object should rotate depending on where it is on the screen. (i.e. "Rotate2", or "Rotate5" etc.)

Bones with ScrollA, ScrollB, ScrollC, or ScrollD in their names (or their parent's names) use a flat scroll. Most BG objects are binded to these bones. And their scroll speed depend on their letter. A is fastest, D is slowest iirc.
