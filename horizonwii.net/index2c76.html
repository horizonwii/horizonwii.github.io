3.Input two Loop point (Advice Use brawlbox try best point,too)\
4.Click "Build" Button,You will find a BUILD.BAT in directory
5.Open "Build.bat" and wait
6.View you brstm in "out" folder,Filename Defauit is "Default.brstm"
