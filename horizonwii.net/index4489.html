 This is my level for the original NSMBW called Dark Water Cave. The level is kinda haunted and water cave themed.
It includes 3 starcoins like always and no custom tilesets. I builded completely from scratch and I think it turned out really good!

Mario enters a haunted water cavern to find some coins for him.

You need to take a dangerous lift across the dangerous waters full with Fish Bones.

Mario goes underwater to escape the cavern.

Swim up to get out of the dark cave!
