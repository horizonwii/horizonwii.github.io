 Credits go to the Knowledge Base Team and me

Method 1 : Reggie
While this is unprofessional, it's the quickest method and it doesn't require any extra programs.

Simply open Reggie! and click the Screenshot button, choose the part/s of the level you want to capture and you're done.

Method 2 : Dolphin
This method is both quick and the best-looking, but it requires a good PC to make the game playable on Dolphin. You can also apply texture packs and various other settings to get much better graphics.

- To make a screenshot, just press F9 or click the "Screenshot" button while emulating. Dolphin will save it in its user directory.

- To make a video, go to Config > Graphics > Advanced and enable Dump Frames. Then go in DSP Settings and enable Dump Audio. This will work only in some cases, so it's best to use a screen recorder, such as FRAPS or Bandicam.

Method 3 : Wii Recording
While this is the most expensive method, it makes the gameplay as easy and vanilla as possible. You will have to buy a capture card (Dazzle, Elgato...), connect the Wii to the TV and the TV to the capture card, which must be connected to the PC.

Everything should be explained in the device's manual. Don't bother buying an HD capture card, as the Wii doesn't support more than 480p.

Hope this helps, and don't forget the other rules for project posting
