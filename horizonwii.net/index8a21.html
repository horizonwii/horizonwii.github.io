 Rolling hills were quite simple to use until NewerSMBW introduced texture edits for them, allowing rolling hills to fit other tilesets that the Newer Team had created. To get these textures, use a uniform size of overworld (NOT 2 BLOCKS!). Next, adjust the size increase option. It will not increase the size (it only increases the size on 2 blocks). Instead, it will change the texture of the rolling hill. A list of all of the rolling hill textures with their corresponding "sizes" is below.

Zero: Normal Grasslands
One: Light Grass (Bombard Cliffs)
Two: Flagstones with Graveyard
Three: Purple Jungle Temple (With Grass On Top)
Four: Mushroom Mountain
Five: Blue Freezeflame
Six: Desert/Prairie
Seven: Japan (Grass and Smooth Dirt)
Eight: Sky City (Flagstones)

To see what they all look like, test them out yourself, or go to https://www.dropbox.com/sh/i9zfeexv8em0ilr/AAAgcsOR1Cr3cSlnoGuEVs63a?dl=0
to look at all the rolling hills pictures.
