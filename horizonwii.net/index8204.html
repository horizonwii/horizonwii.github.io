Some warnings :
- You may experience washed out colours if playing in HDTV Graphics Mode on real Wii, 50hz/60hz is recommended
- This level is NOT multiplayer-compatible, due to the mechanics it uses

Some bugs you may experience :
- BG glitches if you die in the lava zone
- Sprites may (occasionally) despawn after transitioning
- Small camera bug when entering final boss room
- Audio issues in the final zone (unfixable, sadly)
