 I'm halfway through world 2 and boy oh boy do I need to talk about this mess.

Honestly, the game isn't that good. The levels are forgettable, drawn out, and world 2 has some of the most boring levels I've ever seen (not including the minecart level, that one was straight out frustrating).

Everything is just a rip from Newer Wii. The music, the tilesets, the themes, everything. But that's not even the worst part because that alone isn't bad, the fact is that it's a *watered down* version of Newer Wii. I can't remember one damn level from world 1 or world 2, and I just played them both, and that's because the levels themselves aren't that interesting. A level about up/down moving platforms with no other gimmick can only go for so long before being dull and repetitive, especially when the levels are stretched out. There's another level, first level in the sewer world iirc. That level has literally no merit to even exist. The theme is piranna plants and swoopers, which is alright, you can make an interesting level around that in a sewer and cave world.

...I wish that was the case but it wasn't. The level was so boring I can't even begin to explain why. I swear at parts the level repeated itself but it obviously didn't. There are parts in that level where you just run forward for a couple hundred blocks or so while bats just fly over you. I know I'm not explaining this clearly enough but I don't know how else to put it, play the level yourself and find out.

The minecart level was executed terribly. You aren't given any warnings before stray minecarts are coming towards you, or if theirs a gap to crouch under coming ahead, or if there is a jump that requires jumping over an enemy coming ahead. It's like geometry dash where you have to constantly restart the level and remember where everything is.
Even if you do remember where everything is the level is quite boring, as you are relying off of muscle memory half the time.

I can probably put this game down right now and not experience anything different. I'll most likely just forget any new levels I play anyway so what's the point. But I know I have to beat the game to get a full opinion on it but so far, it's nothing to get excited over.
