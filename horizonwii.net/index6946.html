 This contest is themed around New Super Luigi U's level style, meaning that the levels should have a 100 second time limit. The prize is an exclusive demo of my new hack.

You can have:
New tilesets
New music
New or Newer levels

You can't have:
Code or new sprites
Something that crashes the game

Levels will be judged based upon:
Length (the longer it is the better, just keep it doable in a 100 second time limit)
Originality
Gimmicks
How fun it is.

Good luck! Levels will be judged on July 25th. Submit levels to the depot saying it's for the contest.
