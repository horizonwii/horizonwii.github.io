 Welcome to my project thread !

This is an unofficial patched version of New Super Mario World.

What's New Super Mario World ?
NSMW is a modified version of Newer made by MarioNinja two years ago, with edited maps, levels and songs.

Why is it unofficial ?
After the mod was posted on RVLution, the thread was deleted by the Newer Team, who thought his work was simple plagiarism. Instead, i was fascinated by it, it had pretty good maps, the sounds weren't bad, and the levels had interesting additions which were poorly implemented. I wanted to help the author, but he just insulted me and refused to hear what i had to say. The next day, i got a copy of the game thanks to hostilediplomat, and i started editing it myself without any permission, just for fun. After years have passed and the author disappearing, i decided to put this up for the public.

Original mod features :
- Brand new levels and remixed ones from Newer with updated level previews
- 90 new tilesets to make a better experience
- 15 new backgrounds to give a fresh new look to the original stages
- Remixed maps with tons of additions and a brand new World 9
- Brand new soundtracks

What this patch does :
- Unlike the original, constructive criticism is welcome
- Almost every stage tweaked for better aesthetics and functionality (with updated level previews to reflect the changes)
- Backgrounds actually make sense
- Tweaks to existing tilesets to make them more versatile
- Removes non-sensical map elements
- Updates level and world names to fit the Mario franchise
- Songs actually fit in levels and loop properly

Team :
- customlevelfan78 (Everything)
- This is currently a one-man job, but help is welcome !

Progress :

- Levels (World 1) : 98% (just a few bugs left)
- Levels (World 2) : 85% (two levels don't want to work, some bugs left)
- Levels (World 3) : 95% (some bugs left)
- Levels (World 4) : 80% (a level still crashes, everything else still in testing phase)
- Levels (World 5) : 70% (a level needs to be finished, another one crashes, bugs and testing phase underway)
- Levels (everything else) : 0%
- Layout Edits : 100% (from original mod)
- Logo : 100% (stolen taken from original mod)
- Tilesets : 100% (i have everything i need)
- Backgrounds : 60% (most have been done)
- Maps : 50% (original maps are there, but they need to be remade from scratch and doodads ripped)
- Code Edits : 25% (only level names and messages have been tweaked and are not complete)
- Level Previews : 20% (Currently updated up to World 3)
- Music : 0% (will be the last thing)

Current bugs :
- Loading times are VERY long, probably because of maps. Not sure.
- Music cuts off when switching between worlds (my fix attempt failed)
- A World 5 level point is incorrectly set, pointing to a non-existant level and crashing instantly.
