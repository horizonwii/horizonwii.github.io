BRLYT files are layout files. They can contain all sorts of things such as Text, Coordinates (for placing stuff such as Text, or TPL/whatever images), internal RGBA color values (for the colors of stuff such as Text, or TPL/whatever images}, and much more things.

BRLAN files are layout animation files. They are usually used for animating a group of things in a layout (for example, multiple TPL/whatever images).
