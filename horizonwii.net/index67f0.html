To convert the BRLYT to XMLYT with Benzin, you must type the command, in the "Windows command prompt" (or the "Terminal", if you're on Linux), or errors will occur:
benzin r <BRLYTname> <XMLYTname>

Or to convert the XMLYT to BRLYT, you must type the command, in the "Windows command prompt" (or the "Terminal", if you're on Linux), or errors will occur:
benzin m <XMLYTname> <BRLYTname>

Show me the exact command you typed when using Benzin to convert the BRLYT to XMLYT, or the XMLYT to BRLYT.
