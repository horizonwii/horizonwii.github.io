Posted by Mayro
It's pretty simple really - put the contents of a zip on an SD card.
Make sure to put it on the root of the SD card or USB device.

Just keep in mind that there should be riivolution folder (with an XML file) and the hack's patch folder (where all the hack's files/etc. are at) in the ZIP file or whatever of the hack, if you want to play it with Riivolution.

If there isn't an riivolution folder (with an XML file) in the ZIP file or whatever, of the hack and just the data files or such, then it's possible that the hack isn't designed for use with Riivolution.

Because ISOs are not easy to play. They are high in filesize, require something to be installed that can brick your Wii, and it would be more complicated to do this rather than use RVlution. Sure, the patch would be relatively small, but the ISO generated after would be 4 gigabytes of space or more, which takes forever to copy, move, etc. It also would mean you would have to have a big USB too to have many mods. RVlution may not be the greatest thing in the world, but in the long run, it's much more efficient and easier then dealing with ISOs.
