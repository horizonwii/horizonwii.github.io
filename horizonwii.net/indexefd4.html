 The level depo is a place to share your levels to the public easily.

If you're unfamiliar with it, then let me tell fill you in.


The depo can be sorted by theme, size, and even game. When creating your upload, you must insert in those fields.


The list of themes is very expensive, so check that out in due time. Sizes, however, include small, medium, large, and extra large. Games include NSMBW, NewerSMBW, and NSMBU.

After giving your level a nice description, a theme, size, and game, it's time to actually upload your level. To do this, visit Mediafire or Dropbox, upload, and get the link. Now put that link into the link field and you're good.


Once you've posted, your level is out into the public! Here's an example of how that might look:


From here, users can rate your level by either liking/disliking your level. As you can see, this level has 3 likes and 0 dislikes.


You can also search through uploads using a specific theme, length, and game or flip through the pages of the depo.



Coming soon is a following/replies feature. When those are added this post will be updated.

Other than that, that's about it for the depo. Make sure to check it out!
