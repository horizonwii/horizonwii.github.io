New Super Mario Bros. Wii/U Hacking Contest



Hey everyone, I've decided to make a hacking contest to re-boot interest in New Super Mario Bros. Wii / New Super Mario Bros. U hacking! The contestants will follow a given theme (below), and they are required to make a level within a certain amount of time and under certain rules, when the deadline (also below) is reached, each contestant's level will be scored on a given scale.


The deadline for your submissions will be on 11:59PM EST August 2nd, 2017.


The theme for this contest is:

Fire!


This theme may be obscure, so you have to think of tricky ways to amuse a player as they can go through a variety of castles/towers, firey landscapes, and even more! Let your imagination flow. As long as your level fits the above theme, you should be fine. You can use custom music, code, tilesets, go crazy.

Here are the rules:
  1. Your submission must be one level. It can include all of the files that you wish for it to use, which can be tilesets, music, and code.
  2. Your level should be a NSMBW/Newer level, or a NSMBU level
  3. Your level should have a midway point.
  4. Your level should have 3 functional star coins.
  5. Your level should not have anything that can cause glitches or crashes. Glitches can have points removed, crashes will result in a instant DQ.
  6. Your level must fit the theme. Any levels that fail this requirement will be DQ.
  7. And of course, you level must be beatable.

Our 3 judges will be myself, Fruit Smasher, and Luzifer. The levels will be judged on these categories:

Your levels will be judged on 4 categories:
  1. Functionality: Are there any glitches or bugs that can annoy the player?
  2. Level Design: Is the level fun to play? Is it something new?
  3. Theme: How is the given theme executed? Does the level follow the theme well?
  4. Appeal: Is the level well-decorated with assets? Does it feel barren?

And that's all. Level submissions go Here (and please make sure to select "submit to the contest" so your level is recognized as a contest entry). Prizes may be announced depending on the submission counts. Feel free to ask any questions here as well... and good luck!
