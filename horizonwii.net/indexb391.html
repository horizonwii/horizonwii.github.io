<source>
HTML5 tag are used to specify the audio or video file along with it's "MIME type" inside the HTML5
<audio>
or
<video>
tags (the MIME types include "audio/ogg", "audio/mpeg", "audio/wav" for the HTML5 audio tag, and "video/ogg", "video/webm", "video/mp4" for the HTML5 video tag).
