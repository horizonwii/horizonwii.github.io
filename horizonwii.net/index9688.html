Super Mario Bros. Next is a non-profit, fan created modification of the game New Super Mario Bros. 2.
We aim for quality, and will try to make the levels as enjoyable and fun as any other NSMB game.

This project is not abandoned, and the team is hard at work producing new content for the community.
We will update this thread whenever we have a major update on the status of development.

















Demo v2.0 has currently been released!
This demo contains a one world preview only!

To play it, follow the steps below:

To download the mod, visit a certain spreadsheet showing progress, go to the very last tab that says ClickMe.

(Alternatively, check the Discord server, it may have something.. )
