Be aware that the Map files (.kpbin) and Map Tileset files (.bin) in the NewerSMBW mod, will not open in Koopatlas (since Koopatlas only reads .kpmap files for the Maps, and .arc files for the Map Tilesets).

So, you'll have to download the .kpmap Map files here, and .arc Map Tileset files here, in order to use the NewerSMBW Maps and Map Tilesets with Koopatlas.
